My Skills

Modeling & Simulations

Generation of static and dynamic surfaces through manual-modeling, procedural generation, and physical-simulations.

Texturing & Lighting

Layering physical attributes on the surfaces that represent specific materials. This can be achieved with real texture maps, manually painted masks and values, and procedural generation.

Setting up a combination of lights to make the shapes and materials in the scene visible, pronounce the details, and create the intended look.

Animation

Setting objects, lights and cameras in motion, to follow the script and visually tell the desired story.

Compositing & Grading

Merging of the image-output from the 3D-renders and postprocessing.

Tweaking of the colors and distribution of the brighness values, to create a visually well-balanced, and hight-quallity product.
