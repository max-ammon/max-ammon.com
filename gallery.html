Gallery

In this gallery you will find some concepts, unfinished and finished personal projects and assets, both older and current.
To view the content in full resolution, you can just click on it!

Depending on your browser, operating-system and hardware, the web-player may display the P3-D65 colorspace and 10bit color-depth incorrectly.

By clicking on the small arrows:
you can download the content uncompressed in rec2020 colors with 10bit depth(.mxf/DNxHQX), you will also find one sRGB and one P3-D65 version(.mp4/h.265).
By using a color-managed local player, you will get the most accurate representation of the colors.
